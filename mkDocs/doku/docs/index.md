# Welcome to the Hop2School Help Documentation

Hop2School is the smart solution for sustainable and efficient carpooling to school.  
Our software helps parents, students, and schools easily organize carpools,  
plan routes, and manage trips in real time.

With Hop2School, you not only help reduce traffic congestion and lower travel costs,  
but also contribute to protecting the environment. By sharing rides, we reduce CO₂ emissions,  
ease morning traffic around schools, and promote more eco-friendly mobility.

This documentation is designed to help you get the most out of Hop2School. Whether you're looking for a ride,  
organizing a carpool, or handling administrative tasks – you'll find all the important information and guidance here.

**Use Hop2School and make the school commute more sustainable and cost-efficient!**
