# Miscellaneous

## Email:
For many actions, you will receive emails to confirm important steps or to inform you about changes and cancellations. These emails keep you up to date and ensure smooth communication.

## Chats:
The chat feature acts as a management system that displays all your previous conversations. This allows you to access information from past chats at any time. If you no longer want to see a chat, you can click the **X icon** to remove it. If you do not want to receive notifications for a specific chat, you can click the **microphone icon** button to mute the conversation.

## Language:
In the top navigation bar, you can change the language of the website. This translates the page content into the selected language, making it accessible to more users.

## Dark/Light Mode:
In the top navigation bar, there is an icon that allows you to switch between dark mode and light mode. This lets you choose the display mode that best suits your preferences and environment.
