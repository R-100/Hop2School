# Dashboard

The Hop2School dashboard provides a quick overview of upcoming trips, carpooling statistics, and the associated savings. Here are the key sections and their functions:

## 1. Left Side: Trip Overview
- **Top:** An interactive map displays all upcoming trips as markers, helping you keep track of your planned rides.
- **Below:** A list of upcoming trips with the following features:
  - **Chat Button (speech bubble icon):** Direct access to the related chat to coordinate details with other passengers.
  - **Cancel Button (cross icon):** Allows you to quickly cancel a trip if your plans change.

## 2. Top Right: Offer and Acceptance Statistics
A chart shows how often you offer rides and how often you accept rides from others. This helps you analyze and optimize your travel behavior.

## 3. Center: Savings Chart (in Euros)
This chart displays your daily savings from carpooling:

- **Saved travel costs:** Calculated based on shared trips.
- **Additional earnings:** Income from taking other passengers with you.

## 4. Bottom Right: Environmental Statistics
This section visualizes how your carpooling contributes to environmental protection:
- **CO₂ savings** through shared rides.
- **Reduced individual traffic**, helping decrease congestion and air pollution.

!!! tip "Goal"
    With these features, the Hop2School dashboard provides an efficient way to organize rides, save money, and protect the environment at the same time.
